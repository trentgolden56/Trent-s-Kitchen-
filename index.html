    Welcome to my kitchen!

    Hello! My name is Trent. I grew up in Thousand Oaks, California and recently moved to Japan after living my entire life in the US. I first fell in love with data analytics as a sophomore in high school. I was able to mix my passion for data analytics with my love for the game of baseball. I was fascinated at how I could uncover upcoming stars who none of my friends had even heard of at the time, make meaningful adjustments to my own performances, and forecast future events using the data that I had available to me. This ability to make data driven decisions and uncover growth opportunities followed me throughout my professional career. When I worked at Uniqlo, I tracked monthly sales data to help achieve company sales goals. My following job at Senju Sprinkler gave me a new opportunity to explore my love for data analysis through analyzing marketing trends on our Wordpress and Shopify online stores as well as creating data reports for each region in the sales department. Let's connect and explore how we can turn data into actionable insights, and unlock the power of data together!
